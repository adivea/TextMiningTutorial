The schedule loosely follows the Library Carpentry curriculum. Carpentries teach fundamental computing skills for non-programmeers in a learn-by-doing and learn-by-observation approach.

Hands-on approach includes a reliance on the data-science notion of hacking (not cracking). Learn best by experimenting and by working on your own project.

Data in Spreadsheets

Although huge quantities of data today are born digitally, most of us (including programming wizards) still work with data in spreadsheets. Handling and managing spreadsheets is the bread and butter of data work today, so we'll look over and brainstorm some tipcs to avoid unnecessary blunders.
